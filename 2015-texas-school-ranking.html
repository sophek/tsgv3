2015 Texas School Ranking

CHILDREN AT RISK is proud to be releasing the 10th annual School Rankings across the state of Texas. Our School Rankings strive to serve as an accessible guide for parents, educators, and community members on the performance of local schools and to spark important community dialogue on the quality of public education in Texas.

Disclaimer

CHILDREN AT RISK is a nonprofit organization that drives change for children through research, education and influencing public policy. Texas School Guide has been developed independently, and is not endorsed by the Dallas or Fort Worth Independent School Districts. The school level information that appears in this guide has been gathered from the Texas Education Agency, the Dallas and Fort Worth Independent School Districts’ websites, or individual schools. The information in this guide is subject to change at any time and it is important that parents, community members, educators, and all others contact their schools directly to obtain the most current and accurate information.
